My name is Kirby Punay. I am a graduate of San Beda College Alabang with a Degree in Business Management Major in Entrepreneurship. After graduating I worked in Teletech as a Technical Support Representative for a few years before leaving and going back to school for further studies. After a brief stint in school I decided to work again. I joined Convergys as a Customer Support Representative for a year before joining my current employer, Synchrony Financial.

Due to being tired of my work and stagnant, repetitive life in the BPO industry I decided to take programming lessons in the hopes of making a carerr shift and get a new path in life. My siblings were also programmers. This much exposure to the field made the decision to switch that much easier for me.

As you may now have realized, I am new to this industry, and as such my experience is very much limited. But just like in other fields, we always start from the bottom and we build on it as we go along and experience the field. I hope that oneday I could become one of the best programmers in the industry. However I can not do this on my own, I will need your help to succeed. Will you help me, help you?
